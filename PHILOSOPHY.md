This portfolio is built on a core principle of **"Rigorous Play."** 
While many demonstrations use simplified models ("Toy Models"), they are mathematically faithful to the underlying principles of their respective fields (Quantum Mechanics, General Relativity, Chaos Theory).

We explicitly distinguish between:
1.  **Production Simulation**: High-fidelity, matched-filtering, Hamiltonian-accurate engineering (e.g., `Laika SETI` using real data).
2.  **Pedagogical Models**: Geometric intuitions that sacrifice details for clarity (e.g., `Amplituhedron Toy`, `Toy Spin-Lattice`).
3.  **Semi-Classical Intuition**: Visualization bridges where full Quantum Gravity is intractable (e.g., `Black Hole Raytracer`).
